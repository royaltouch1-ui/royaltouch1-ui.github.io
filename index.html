About Us

Royal Touch Tech is a data analytics, accounting, and business intelligence firm focused on helping businesses make informed financial and operational decisions.

We combine strong analytical skills with practical accounting knowledge to transform raw financial and business data into clear insights, accurate records, and meaningful reports.

Our expertise spans data analysis, bookkeeping, financial reporting, and the use of modern accounting systems such as Sage 50 Accounting and QuickBooks to support small and growing businesses.

Our mission is to simplify accounting and data analysis for businesses and students by providing reliable financial records, professional reports, and data-driven recommendations.
